About Me

Protecting Digital Assets Through Ethical Hacking

With over 1 years of experience in cybersecurity, I specialize in identifying and addressing security vulnerabilities before they can be exploited by malicious actors. My approach combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of business risks.

I hold multiple certifications including CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker), CISSP, and OSCP. My work has helped organizations across various industries strengthen their security posture and protect sensitive data.

I believe in transparency, ethical practices, and empowering organizations with the knowledge to maintain their security long after our engagement ends.
